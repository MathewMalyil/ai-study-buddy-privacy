Privacy Policy & Data Deletion — AI Study Buddy

Developer: Mathew Malyil

Effective Date: [Insert Date]

Summary: AI Study Buddy does not require user accounts. Data is processed locally or temporarily for AI responses. Users can delete local data by uninstalling the app. For any additional requests, contact malyilmathew@gmail.com.

1. Information We Collect

  • User Input & Files: Text you enter and documents/images you choose to analyze (PDF, DOCX, PPTX, XLSX, CSV, images). These are sent securely to AI services to generate responses.
  • Usage Data: Local counters for token usage, subscription status, and error info to improve reliability.
  • Device/Crash Data: Basic non-identifiable info (OS version, app version, crash logs) for debugging.
  • Billing: Purchases/subscriptions are handled by Google Play Billing. The app does not store card details.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide AI-powered study assistance and generate responses.
  • Track usage against free and Pro limits.
  • Improve performance, reliability, and user experience.
  • Comply with legal and billing requirements.

3. Data Sharing

  • AI APIs: Prompts/files are processed by AI providers (e.g., OpenAI, Groq) strictly to generate responses.
  • Google Play Services: For billing and crash reporting.
  • No sale or rental of personal data. No advertising networks receive your personal data.

4. Data Retention

  • User input and files are not stored permanently on our servers; they are processed transiently to return results.
  • Usage counters and preferences are stored locally on your device.
  • You can reset or uninstall the app to clear local data.

5. Children’s Privacy

AI Study Buddy is intended for users 18+ and is not directed to children. If we learn that data from minors was collected inadvertently, we will delete it promptly.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, update, or delete your data. Contact us to make a request.

7. Security

Data is encrypted in transit over HTTPS. No security method is perfect, but we implement industry-standard safeguards.

8. Data Deletion (Google Play Requirement)

AI Study Buddy does not require user accounts. To control or delete your data:

  • Local app data (usage counters, preferences): Uninstall the app from your device.
  • Uploaded content (prompts, documents): Processed temporarily by AI services to generate responses and not stored permanently.
  • Subscriptions: Managed by Google Play. Cancel anytime in your Google Play account.

Additional deletion requests: Email malyilmathew@gmail.com with your device model, app version, and approximate usage time so we can locate and purge any transient logs (if applicable).

What is deleted vs kept

  • Deleted: Local usage data on uninstall; transient processing data not required for security/fraud prevention.
  • Kept: Google Play purchase records (held by Google); aggregated, non-identifiable analytics (if any).
  • Retention: Transient server logs (if any) are automatically purged within 30 days.
